What is the structure of your dataset?¶

There are 113,937 loan data in dataset with 81 columns. Most variables are numeric. However few vaiables are categorical like

  • Loan Status (Completed, Current, Past Due, Defaulted)
  • Borrwer State
  • Occupation
  • Employmenet Status (self-employed, Employed)
  • etc.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

I am interested to figure out

  • what features are best to predict whether the loan will be defaulted.
  • what are factors contributed to low borrow rate i.e. high credit rate, high salary, etc?

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

I believed that loan defualted or not should be related to income level, debt to equity ratio (D/E), credit grade, occupation. While lower borrowing rate should come from something like higher income level.
